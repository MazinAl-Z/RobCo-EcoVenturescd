Robert Edwin House – CEO of the Century

Born June 25, 2020, Robert House founded RobCo Industries in his garage at age 22. By 2075, he had become the wealthiest and most respected industrialist in American history. A graduate of the Commonwealth Institute of Technology, Mr. House personally designed the Unified Operating System that runs 97% of the nation's terminals, as well as the pioneering life‑support systems that will allow him to guide humanity for centuries to come. His vision? A future of zero‑emission robotics, fusion‑powered luxury travel, and American dominance in every technological field. The EcoVentures division is his latest gift to a grateful nation.

REPCONN Aerospace – A RobCo Company

Since RobCo's friendly acquisition of REPCONN in 2075, the combined entity has become the undisputed leader in civilian rocketry, plasma propulsion, and automated transportation. Under Mr. House's guidance, REPCONN now builds the vertibird fleet that powers EcoVentures – silent, fusion‑powered, and utterly safe. Every component is blessed by House Industries quality assurance. Whether you're soaring above the Grand Canyon or docking at the Lucky 38's private launchpad, you're experiencing the genius of RobCo. America trusts no other name.
